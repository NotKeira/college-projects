"Don't Tell the Dog" Hits Number 1

June 2025

Celebrating an incredible milestone! My second album "Don't Tell the Dog" has reached Number 1 on the UK Albums Chart. Thank you to everyone who supported this deeply personal project about my childhood and growth as an artist.

This album represents a significant step forward in my musical journey, exploring themes of nostalgia, family, and the bittersweet nature of growing up. Each track tells a story, and I'm overwhelmed by the response from fans who've connected with these deeply personal songs.

From "I Don't Want to Live Like This" to "Toothache," every song on this album comes from a real place in my heart. The fact that it's reached Number 1 means the world to me and shows that authentic storytelling still resonates with people.

Rodd's Coffee Brand Launch

May 2025

Teaming up with my mate WillNE to launch Rodd's - our new iced coffee brand with three unique flavours. It's been amazing branching into entrepreneurship whilst continuing to create music that matters to me.

Rodd's isn't just another celebrity coffee brand - it's something we're genuinely passionate about. We've spent months perfecting the recipes and working with ethical suppliers to create something we're proud to put our names on.

The three launch flavours represent different aspects of our personalities and tastes. From the bold espresso blend to the smooth vanilla variant, there's something for every coffee lover.

You can find Rodd's in select retailers across the UK, with online ordering available soon. It's been incredible seeing our vision come to life!
